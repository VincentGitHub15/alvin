About Alvin

Alvin Healthcare is a subscription service for tele-access of doctors and nurses. Clients are usually those with a high insurance deductible or are otherwise uninsured. Alvin Healthcare approached us with a feature that they would like to see implemented on their site in the future. This app is a mockup to show potential investors of Alvin, and is ready to be scaled out. You can find out more about Alvin Healthcare at helloalvin.com.
