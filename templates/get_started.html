Get Started

What's in the database:

4 Relational Tables:

  • Facilities and Addresses
  • Geolocations of Facilities
  • Procedures and Descriptions
  • Pricing of Procedures at Facilities

We currently have very limited data in the database because finding public medical pricing data is nearly impossible.
That being said, we seeded our database with 8 total facilities and their current locations, most of which are in Manhattan.
If you are using this map outside of NYC, do not turn accept the google maps location prompt, you will likely not see any facilities near you.

We have a total of 4 medical procedures that can be searched.
You may start typing in the search bar, and it will populate with any matches in the database.
To be precise, these are the procedures and their CPT (current procedure terminology) codes that are in the database:

  • CT Head W W/O Contrast, 70470
  • MRI Brain W W/O Contrast, 70553
  • X-Ray Ankle 3 views, 73610
  • Total Knee Arthroplasty, 71005
Upon clicking each marker that displays, you may notice that the pricing data for these procedures seem very expensive. Our intention for this project is just to display a place holder for the price of the procedure at that specific location. Our application and database is ready to scale and will be much more powerful when given accurate data.

Looking Ahead

Future features that we would like to incorporate into the application include:

  • Markers that display the price, without having to click to open
  • Additional search filters: price, distance/radius, facility rating
  • Ability for users to log in and save their searches
  • More data
