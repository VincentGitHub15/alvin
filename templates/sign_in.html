There is a lack of platform that allows patients to look for cost effective solutions for medical services as prescribed by their doctors. Patients should be able to search for a requested procedure and the app should return facilities that provide that service. Patients would then be able to filter by location/distance, price, and rating; something that will be implemented in the future when more data is available.

Specfically, Alvin clients would also be able to log in and save their searches so they can easily return with their preferences stored.
